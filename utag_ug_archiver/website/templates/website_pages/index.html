Our Core Values

With these values, we embrace our mission to bring each client the best service.

Quality Education

We are committed to defending and promoting academic freedom, ensuring that scholars can pursue research, teaching, and other academic activities without undue external interference.

Advance Teaching and Research

We are committed to advancing teaching and research excellence. We strive to enhance standards, and researchers to create a brighter future through education.

Advocate for Membership

We advocate for the needs and concerns of our membership to the University Management, the leadership of the National Association, and the Government of the Republic.

About Us

The University of Ghana Branch of the University Teachers Association of Ghana (UG-UTAG) is an organization representing the teaching and research staff of the University of Ghana. Its primary purpose is to promote the academic, professional, and economic welfare of its members, as well as the welfare of the entire academic community of the University of Ghana.
